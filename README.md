# ReBlock v1 (mc 26.2)

Craft full blocks back out of their slabs. Put 2 matching slabs on top of each
other in any crafting grid (inventory 2x2 works) and get the full block back.
It's the reverse of the vanilla slab recipe: 1 block splits into 2 slabs, so 2
slabs recombine into 1 block.

## Download

**[⬇ ReBlock.v1_mc_26.2.zip](https://github.com/HeyItzAine/ReBlock/raw/main/ReBlock.v1_mc_26.2.zip)**

## Install

Drop the zip into the world's `datapacks/` folder and `/reload` (or restart the
server). A one-time chat message confirms the pack loaded. Reloads and updates
after that stay silent.

## What's craftable

61 recipes, covering every vanilla slab that has a full-block counterpart:

- **Wood:** all 12 plank types (oak, spruce, birch, jungle, acacia, dark oak,
  mangrove, cherry, pale oak, bamboo, crimson, warped) plus bamboo mosaic
- **Stone:** stone, smooth stone, cobblestone, mossy cobblestone, stone bricks,
  mossy stone bricks, granite, diorite, andesite (+ polished variants)
- **Deepslate:** cobbled, polished, bricks, tiles
- **Tuff:** tuff, polished tuff, tuff bricks
- **Sandstone:** regular, cut, smooth, in both yellow and red
- **Nether/End:** nether bricks, red nether bricks, quartz, smooth quartz,
  purpur, end stone bricks, blackstone, polished blackstone, polished
  blackstone bricks
- **Copper:** all 4 cut copper oxidation stages, waxed and unwaxed
- **Misc:** bricks, mud bricks, prismarine, prismarine bricks, dark prismarine,
  resin bricks

The petrified oak slab is excluded. It has no full-block counterpart in
vanilla.

## Notes

- Recipes appear in the recipe book as soon as you have any slab in your
  inventory.
- 2 slabs make 1 block. Nothing is gained or lost converting back and forth.
- Slabs must match. 2 oak slabs make oak planks; mixing types doesn't craft.
